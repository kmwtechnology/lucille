Activity
Canadian Natural Resources Limited has submitted an application under the Environmental Protection and Enhancement Act for a renewal of an existing approval for the Ante Creek sour gas plant. The facility is located in LSD 10 of Section 18 Township 65 Range 23 West of the 5th Meridian (approximately 22 km northeast of Little Smoky) and operates as a battery only. The raw gas process capacity of the plant is 350 × 103 m3/day at 101.325 kPa and 15°C, including raw gas and condensate. The deadline for submitting a statement of concern is December 2, 2015.
